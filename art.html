Crowd2Map Tanzania

As a volunteer with Crowd2Map Tanzania, I actively contribute to mapping projects focused on supporting sustainable development in rural areas. Our primary objective is to map areas at risk of female genital mutilation (FGM) using crowdsourcing and open data. Through the identification and mapping of buildings and road networks from satellite images, we create detailed maps on OpenStreetMap. These maps serve as valuable resources for outreach efforts and help guide girls to safety. Being part of Crowd2Map Tanzania has allowed me to witness the transformative power of crowdsourcing and open data in driving positive change. I take pride in my role and remain committed to contributing to the organization's mission of sustainable development through mapping and community engagement.
