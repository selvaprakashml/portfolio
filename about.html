I am devoted to continual learning and professional growth in the GIS sector, and I try to increase productivity by using my project management, problem-solving, and process improvement skills. I hope to create creative solutions that improve the accuracy, quality, and usefulness of geographic information by utilizing my expertise in photogrammetry, BIM, geometric technology, and spatial statistics. After a year, I decided to resign from my job because of not being able to do the night shift throughout the year.
